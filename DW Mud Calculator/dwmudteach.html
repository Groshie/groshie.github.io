Discworld MUD Teaching Cost Calculator

Made by: Groshie (2025)

Welcome, follow the steps below to start calculating your journey to greatness!

This tool aims to follow in the footsteps of http://bonuses.irreducible.org when it comes to teaching xp cost. The rest of the tools on that site should still be up to date!

The formula has been adapted from this page (Unofficial Discworld MUD Wiki).

Your stats

Your (raw) stats are needed to calculate the total cost because your bonus in the skill has an effect on the result.

The skill

Choose which skill you are learning. It's relevant since skills use different stats for calculating bonus.

The rest

Here we need your current level, which level you want to reach, the effective teaching bonus (ETB) of the teacher, and if the teacher is specialised. These are all used in the xp cost formula.
